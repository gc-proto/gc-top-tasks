Your NSLSC account

Manage your loan, make payments and apply for repayment help from your NSLSC account.

Login to your NSLSC account

Keep your loan payment-free while you study

Let us know that you are still in study. You can:

  • log in to your NSLSC account to confirm your enrolment
  • submit a paper copy of the Confirmation of Enrolment (PDF, 267 KB)

This will make sure that repayment is delayed until you finish.

Medical and parental leave

If you need to take time away from your studies for medical or parental reasons, you can apply for Medical and parental leave.

Let another person manage your student loan

You can give another person power of attorney to take care of your student loan dealings with us.

  • Power of Attorney form (PDF, 69 KB) 

Fill out the form and send it to:

National Student Loans Service Centre (NSLSC)
P.O. Box 4030 
Mississauga ON  L5A 4M4

Reservists in the Canadian Forces

If you are a reservist in the Canadian Forces on a designated operation you can delay repayment on your student loan.
