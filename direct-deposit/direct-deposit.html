Direct deposit

Get payments from the Government of Canada deposited directly into your bank account.

You can:

  • Set up direct deposit
  • Change your payments to a different bank account
  • Report a late, incorrect or unexpected payment (best done by phone)

If you are receiving multiple payments, you may need to contact different government departments to update your information.

Services and information

Individuals in Canada

Tax refunds and credits, benefits, retirement pensions, Employment Insurance, student grants and loans

Individuals outside of Canada

Check available countries and payments, sign up or chage banking information

Businesses and other organizations

Direct deposit for payment of supplier invoices, business tax refunds, grants and contributions
