Direct deposit

Get payments from the Government of Canada deposited directly into your bank account. If you are receiving multiple payments, you may need to contact different government departments to update your information.

You can:

  • Set up direct deposit
  • Change your payments to a different bank account
  • Report a late, incorrect or unexpected payment (best done by phone)
